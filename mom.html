Mom

My mom was born on January 16th 1977. She was born in Queens, New York, and would spend the first decade of her life there, before she would move to Long Island,New York. She moved a few houses down from where my dad lived. She would go to Union College, which is a college in Schenectady New York, which is right next to Albany New York. She would go to Medical school to become a pediatrician. After that she would get a job as a pediatrician on Long Island New York
